📈 Detection Statistics

📖 Metrics Explained:
  • Radius: Distance from center to edge of detected circle (in pixels)
  • Mean Value: Average pixel intensity inside the circle (16-bit: 0-65535). Lower = darker, Higher = brighter
  • Circularity: How round the shape is (0.0-1.0). 1.0 = perfect circle, lower = more irregular
  • Solidity: How solid/filled the shape is (0.0-1.0). 1.0 = completely solid, lower = has holes or concave areas
